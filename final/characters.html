Characters

This page will show you details on the most popular and reacurring characters of FNaF.

Freddy Fazbear

Freddy Fazbear is the main star and antagonist of Five Nights at Freddy's. He serves as the main mascot of the franchise. Freddy Fazbear consistantly appears in almost every game in the series. He is possesed by the child spirit, Gabriel. When Gabriel attented his friend's birthday party at Freddy Fazbear's pizzaria, he and 4 other children were lured into a room by William Afton and got murdered. Gabriel got stuffed into Freddy Fazbear, possesing him. In the first game, Freddy isn't very active until the later nights. He is stealthy and tries to hide from the cameras, as well as laugh at you whenever he moves.

Bonnie

Bonnie is another antagonist of Five Nights at Freddy's. He is possesed by the child spirit, Jeremy. When Jeremy attented his friend's birthday party at Freddy Fazbear's pizzaria, he and 4 other children were lured into a room by William Afton and got murdered. Jeremy was stuffed into the Bonnie animatronic, possesing him. In the games, Bonnie is usually the most agressive out of the other animatronics. You often encounter a Bonnie first in the first few games.
